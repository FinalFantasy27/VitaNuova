罗尔斯的两条正义原则（rules）：
(i) 每个人都有平等的权利，享有与所有人的类似自由相容的最广泛的基本自由。
(ii) 在整个人口中分配财富和收入等初级产品（无论人们还想要什么，它们都是有利的），以最大限度地提高社会中处境最不利成员的地位，并在机会均等的条件下通过公平竞争获得职位和职位。
(i) Each person is to have an equal right to the most extensive basic liberty compatible with a similar liberty for all. 
(ii) Primary goods like wealth and income (which are advantageous no matter what ever else one may want) are to be distributed throughout the population to maximize the position of the least advantaged members of society, and to be derived from offices and positions filled by fair competition under conditions of equality of opportunity.
