
政治多元论的一个版本以道德价值多元论为基础，声称存在不可还原的多元道德价值，这为自由主义政治制度提供了理由。(See Isaiah Berlin 1969; George Crowder 2002, 2019; William Galston 2002, and for a more detailed discussion of this see the entry on Isaiah Berlin)

Judith Jarvis Thomson (1997), W.D. Ross (1930), Bernard Williams (1981), Charles Taylor (1982), Stuart Hampshire (1983), Charles Larmore (1987), John Kekes (1993), Michael Stocker (1990 and 1997), David Wiggins (1997), Christine Swanton (2001), and Jonathan Dancy (2004) are all committed to foundational pluralism.
