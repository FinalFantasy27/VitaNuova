古希腊哲学家的科学成就：

泰勒斯的观察（可能不是证明 ）

a) 一个圆被它的直径一分为二

b) 两边相等的三角形的底角相等，以及

c) 两边相等、两角相等的三角形本身相等

毕达哥拉斯的门徒的证明

a) 三角形的角之和等于两个直角（公元前 450 年以前）

b) 直角三角形斜边的平方等于其他边的平方和（公元前 450 年以前）

c) 2 的平方根是无理数，即不能用分数表示的数（公元前 450 年）

以及

d) 3、5、7、11、13 和 17 的平方根（与 2 的平方根一样）也是无理数（狄奥多罗斯，普罗泰戈拉的学生和柏拉图的老师，约 400 年）

德谟克利特的发现

a) 圆锥体的体积与同底同高的圆柱体的体积相同，并且

同底同高的圆柱体的体积，以及

b) 金字塔的体积是同底同高棱柱的体积的 ⅓

希波克拉底（约 440 年）证明了

两个圆的面积之比等于直径米的平方之比

天文学观察和假设

a) 阿那克萨哥拉和毕达哥拉斯都推测地球是一个球体，以及

b) 晨星就是晚星

The observations (prob ably not proofs) of Thales 
a) that a circle is bisected by its diameter,
b) that the angles at the base of a triangle with two equal sides are equal, and
c) that triangles with an equal side and two equal angles are themselves equal.
The proofs by followers of Pythagoras
a) that the sum of the angles of a triangle are equal to two right angles (prior to 450 BCE),
b) that the square of the hypotenuse of a right triangle is equal to the sum of the squares of the other sides (prior to 450),
c) that the square root of 2 is irrational, i.e., a number that can’t be expressed as a fraction (prior to 450),
and
d) that the square roots of 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, and 17 are (like that of 2) also irrational (Theodorus, pupil of Protagoras and teacher of Plato, circa 400).
The discoveries by Democritus
a) that the volume of a cone is that of a cylinder
with the same base and height, and
b) that the volume of a pyramid is ⅓ that of a prism with the same base and height.
The proof by Hippocrates (circa 440) that the ratio of
the areas of two circles equals the ratios of the squares of their dia meters.
The astronomical observations and hypotheses
a) that the earth is a sphere, conjectured by both Anaxagoras and Pythagoras, and
b) that the Morning Star is the Evening Star.
