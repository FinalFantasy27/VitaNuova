Achilles on Skyros基斯罗斯岛
The episode is not existing in Homer's epic poem Iliad.
Achilles disguised himself as a girl, under the name Pyrrha "the red-haired"（伪装成公主）, Issa, or Kerkysera, at the court of the king of Skyros, fell in love with one of the princesses, and married her before leaving for Troy.
